Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required to enroll?

To enroll, you will need to provide a copy of your child’s birth certificate or passport. You will also need to provide proof of address. This can be in the form of a utility bill, bank statement, or other official document dated within the last 3 months. Alternatively, you may provide a copy of your driving license or council tax bill.

Can I get a free trial?

We do not offer free trials, but we do hold regular online open events and taster lessons throughout the year. These sessions are a great way to get a feel for what it’s like to learn at Elite Learning Center.

Can a student catch up with missed work if joining later in the term?

Yes, students who join us in the middle of a term will have access to previous learning materials. This will help them to catch up independently outside of timetabled lessons.

Can my child study subjects across year groups?

Yes, it is possible to study different subjects across different year groups. However, timetables may clash. If lesson clashes do occur, students can use lesson recordings to catch up.
